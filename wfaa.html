Overview

The Wisconsin Foundation and Alumni Association (WFAA) is a non-profit organization which serves the benefit of the university and its constituents. WFAA's efforts range from hosting events for students and alumni, as well as fundraising to help support the university's research, students, and faculty. My wowk with the organization began in 2019 as a Digital Experience Specialist. I built email journeys and web content to help support marketing efforts for events and fundraising campaigns. As I continued to progress in my career, I developed an interest in User Experience Design. After expressing this interest, I began to take UX-related tasks and assignments. After some time of doing that, my position as Senior Digital Experience Specialist evolved from email marketing to only user expereience, reseearch, and web content design & creation.

In the last three years, I've had a hand in designing websites from scratch, low fidelity and high fidelity wireframing, creating user stories, user research, and maintaining quality of life of our products in regards to content management and design. *ADD MORE HERE*
